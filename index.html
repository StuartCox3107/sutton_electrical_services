Sutton Electrical Services
Friendly and reliable service

Covering all of Suffolk, Norfolk and Cambridgeshire for all domestic, commercial and industrial work.

Sutton electrical services have been offering domestic, commercial and residential electrical work since 2016. With 21 years of experience as an electrician, owner Alan provides a high-quality reliable, trustworthy service, with all work finished to a very high standard. Specialising in domestic services, and able to undertake all types of electrical work for you.

Services include
  • New builds
  • Full rewires
  • Extensions
  • Heating systems including smart systems
  • CCTV & security systems
  • Testing & inspections
  • EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Reports)
  • Garden & outdoor installations
